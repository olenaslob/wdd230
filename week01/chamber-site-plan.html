Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Calgary Chamber of Commerce

Reason for selection: This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

"The Carlsbad Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity" - carlsbad.org site description

Another purpose is to foster collaboration among local businesses to strengthen the local economy.

Scenarios

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Color (Light): #0066cc - Used for headings and main elements in the light theme.

Primary Color (Dark): #ffcc00 - Used for headings and main elements in the dark theme.

Secondary Color: #333333 - Used for body text in the light theme.

Secondary Color: #ecf0f1 - Used for body text in the dark theme.

Typography

Heading Font (Light Theme): Arial, sans-serif - Used for headings in the light theme.

Heading Font (Dark Theme): 'Times New Roman', serif - Used for headings in the dark theme.

Body Font (Light Theme): Arial, sans-serif - Used for the body text in the light theme.

Body Font (Dark Theme): 'Times New Roman', serif - Used for the body text in the dark theme.
